Hetco Design provides pragmatic answers that work in the real-world. We specialise in international, flexible, swift design research.

We explore new possibilities and then develop the most promising options into viable products, services and systems via mixed methods design research. We can help startups, nonprofits and business organisations to:

  • Identify new opportunities & Understand new situations via discovery research

  • De-risk concepts via alpha experiments

  • Rethink existing systems, products and services via contextual inquiries

Our mixed methods approach combines:

  • human-centred design

  • data science

  • experiment design

  • GIS mapping

  • game design

  • digital and physical rapid prototyping

  • strategy & model thinking

Project lengths vary from last 8 weeks to 6 months. Contact us for project case studies. We offer free consultancy for startups with at least 2 full time employees.
