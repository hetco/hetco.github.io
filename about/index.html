About

Simon Johnson, Data Scientist

Simon has worked with open data communities for the last 7 years in the humanitarian sector. In 2014 Simon joined the Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX), an open data portal and community for the humanitarian community, soon after its initial launch. His role involved increasing community engagement through building data visualisations with identified key partner. In 2016 Simon designed the concept and technical implementations for HDX tools domain which included tools for transforming, validating and visualising open data faster. Simon has sat on the Humanitarian Exchange Language (an open data standard) Working Group for the last 4 years.

Shruti Grover, Human Centred Designer/Researcher

Shruti is a Human Centred Designer/Researcher who has led conception,research, design and delivery of innovative healthcare products and services for companies including Médecins Sans Frontières, Stannah Stairlifts, Proteus Digital Health, Chelsea and Westminster Hospital, Cerestim Therapeutics, Ford, Rio Tinto and the Seoul Design Foundation. Recent achievements include the launch of the Stannah Balance application, a mobile phone application to measure balance and helping Proteus Digital Health pivot to better serve Hepatitis C patients. She holds an MA/ MSc (Dist) in Innovation Design Engineering from the Royal College of Art and Imperial College.
